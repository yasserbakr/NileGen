Life from the Nile

NileGen was founded on the principle that the ancient wisdom of the Nile Valley can inspire modern scientific breakthroughs. Our name reflects this unique fusion of Egyptian heritage and cutting-edge biotechnology.

Located in Egypt, we leverage local expertise and global standards to develop molecular biology products that meet the rigorous demands of researchers worldwide.

Our "Life from the Nile" concept embodies purity, scientific excellence, and a connection to one of humanity's oldest cradles of civilization.
